Registration Instructions for Adult Chaperones

Thank you for your interest in Roslyn Code Overload. We’re so excited to have you and your students as part of our event! Per school policy, all students need to be accompanied by an adult chaperone, which is where you come in. You will make supervise you students, but also have the opportunity to act as a mentor, judge, or even host a workshop if interested.

As the adult chaperone, please follow these steps to make sure your students are successfully registered for the event.

Step 1. Gather Your Students

Find a group of students who are interested in attending Roslyn Code Overload. Students can come from computer science, engineering, enturapernship, or even art classes and clubs. They do not have to be experts in programming.

Step 2. Submit Registration Form

Once you have your group of students, print out and complete the School Registration Form. The form must be signed by a school administration (ex. principal). Once the form is completed, email a copy of it to hello@roslyncode.tech.

Step 3. Await Confirmation

After you emailed the form to us, give us 1-3 business days to process your registration form. Once processed, we will send you a confirmation email.

Step 4. Submit Students Forms

Upon receiving confirmation, have your students fill out the Student Registration Form.

Step 5. Celebrate!

Congratulations, you’ve successfully registered for Roslyn Code Overload! Look out for emails in the two weeks before February 9 for further event details and waiver forms.
